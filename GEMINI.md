## UI/UX Principles
- **Interactive Navigation:** Progress indicators (like steppers) should be interactive and clickable, allowing users to move between completed or accessible steps easily.
- **Dynamic & Responsive Layouts:** Components should handle various screen sizes gracefully without feeling stretched or becoming difficult to interact with.
- **Spatial Optimization:** Minimize vertical scrolling by using compact layouts (e.g., side-by-side fields on larger screens) and reducing unnecessary padding/margins.
- **Visual Feedback:** Provide clear visual cues for interactive states (hover, active, disabled) and loading processes.
